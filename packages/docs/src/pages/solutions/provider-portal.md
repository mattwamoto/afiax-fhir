# Provider and Partner Portal

Afiax supports provider-facing and partner-facing portals for referrals, diagnostics, specialist coordination,
distributed care delivery, and cross-organization collaboration.

## Common use cases

- referring clinician portals
- specialist collaboration workspaces
- diagnostics and results distribution
- payer or partner visibility into controlled workflow data
- shared reporting for distributed provider networks

## Platform capabilities

- **search and access controls:** give each partner the exact data scope they need
- **communications and follow-up:** support provider-to-provider collaboration and referral-loop closure
- **notifications:** trigger email, SMS, and other partner events through Bots
- **documents and results:** share reports and linked records through structured workflows
- **API access:** allow institutions and partners to connect directly to Afiax services when a portal is not enough

## Why this matters

Cross-organization coordination is a core Afiax use case. Provider and partner portals make the platform useful beyond
the four walls of one facility while preserving auditability and access boundaries.
