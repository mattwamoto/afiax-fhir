# Scheduling and Access Coordination

Afiax scheduling supports clinical operations across facilities, programs, outreach teams, and virtual-care models.
It is not only about slot booking. It is part of access management, care coordination, and service delivery.

![Scheduling Art](../products/img/medplum-scheduling.png)

## Common Afiax uses

- in-person clinic scheduling
- telemedicine and virtual-care visits
- multi-location provider calendars
- outreach and community-health visits
- pre-visit intake and readiness checks
- referral-driven scheduling and follow-up

## Platform capabilities

- **schedule and slot management:** represent provider, location, and service availability
- **matching and filtering:** find the right appointment based on service type, geography, credential, or care team
- **pre-visit workflows:** require forms, coverage checks, or other readiness criteria before booking
- **notifications and reminders:** trigger SMS, email, and partner communications via Bots
- **workflow linkage:** connect appointments to encounters, communications, tasks, and downstream exchange

## Why this matters

In African healthcare delivery settings, access is often constrained by geography, staffing, infrastructure, and
referral complexity. Scheduling needs to coordinate those realities rather than just expose a calendar UI.
