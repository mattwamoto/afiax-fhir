# Questionnaires and Structured Data Capture

Afiax uses questionnaires as a platform capability for structured intake, clinical screening, operational workflows,
program enrollment, and patient- or staff-generated data capture.

![Forms image](../products/img/forms.png)

## Common Afiax uses

- registration and onboarding
- triage and symptom capture
- patient-reported outcomes
- referrals and program enrollment
- quality and compliance forms
- workforce and operational questionnaires

## Platform capabilities

- **FHIR-native forms:** use standard `Questionnaire` and `QuestionnaireResponse` resources
- **reusable libraries:** manage versions, categories, and deployment metadata centrally
- **embedded experiences:** surface forms inside provider, patient, and partner applications
- **workflow triggers:** use Bot automation after submission for routing, validation, and follow-up
- **localized rollout:** use country or tenant overlays to adapt form sets without redefining the core model

## Why this matters

Structured data capture is one of the fastest ways to make a healthcare platform usable across facilities, programs,
and markets. Afiax uses questionnaires to keep data capture aligned with the canonical model while allowing local
variation in workflow.
