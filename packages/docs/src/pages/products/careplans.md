# Care Plans and Program Workflows

Afiax uses care plans, tasks, and related workflow resources to coordinate long-running patient and program activity.
This is useful in routine clinical care, chronic disease programs, specialty pathways, and public-health workflows.

## Common Afiax uses

- referral pathways and follow-up
- maternal and child health programs
- chronic disease management
- longitudinal care-team coordination
- diagnostics or treatment pathways
- operational playbooks for facility teams

## Platform capabilities

- **plan design:** represent reusable protocols and pathway logic
- **patient-specific execution:** instantiate plans and track actual progress for a person or case
- **workflow linkage:** connect tasks, forms, appointments, communications, and orders into one pathway
- **team coordination:** assign responsibility across clinicians, coordinators, and operations teams
- **auditability:** keep a traceable record of what was planned, triggered, completed, or escalated

## Why this matters

Care delivery at scale depends on coordinated workflow, not just data storage. Afiax uses care-plan patterns to make
clinical and operational programs executable across providers, facilities, and country contexts.
